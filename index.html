~ About Us ~

Introduce

For over 25 years, VINMERICA GROUP has been a leading manufacturer of high-quality footwear, proudly crafting a diverse range of styles that cater to all walks of life. With our roots deeply embedded in China and Vietnam, we have established ourselves as a reputable and experienced player in the footwear industry. Our dedicated team possesses a wealth of knowledge spanning from sturdy boots to comfortable slides, and from cutting-edge basketball footwear to stylish everyday options

Why Choose Vinmerica

At Vinmerica, our team's expertise covers the entire spectrum of footwear. Whether you're seeking footwear for athletic performance or casual relaxation, our specialists possess the insights and experience to craft the perfect solution. From concept to creation, we are dedicated to ensuring that every pair of shoes we produce embodies the essence of quality and style.


OUR LEGACY OF EXCELLENCE

At VINMERICASHOES, we pride ourselves on being a small yet robust company. This unique attribute empowers us to maintain a close-knit team that embodies a Western design mentality. With a presence firmly rooted in Vietnam, we guarantee a seamless and consistent flow of production, while infusing each design with a touch of sophistication and innovation.
